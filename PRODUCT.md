# CareCompass — Product

## What it is
CareCompass is a mobile-first care coordination web app for a person managing complex, multi-domain medical and mobility needs, plus their assistant/driver. It unifies every thread of care — providers, appointments, referrals, records transfers, prescriptions, wheelchair/equipment, accessible van, and tax-deductible receipts — into one supportive hub that surfaces what needs attention before it becomes urgent.

## Users
- **Persona 1 — The Person in Care** (Samsung Android): lives with a disability/complex chronic condition; multiple specialists, frequent changeable appointments, active referrals, multi-pharmacy prescriptions, a powered wheelchair and an accessible van both needing constant maintenance. Carries heavy cognitive/logistical load. Needs the full picture.
- **Persona 2 — The Assistant / Driver** (iPhone 12): drives the van, handles day-to-day logistics, fuel fill-ups, van maintenance, and captures fuel/maintenance receipts for tax. Needs a task-focused view: today's run, what to pick up, outstanding receipts.

## Core problem
Managing care spans dozens of providers and deadlines. Appointments change without warning, referrals expire, prescriptions need refilling, equipment and vehicle need unpredictable repairs, and deductible receipts get lost. No single place shows the full picture; the whole load falls on one or two people.

## Core solution
A unified companion that holds every thread of care and proactively surfaces what needs attention. Warm, encouraging, calm tone — a capable caring assistant, not a clinical task manager.

## Key features
1. Today Dashboard — today's appointments chronologically, live travel context, "when to be ready".
2. Calendar & Appointment Hub — unified colour-coded timeline (medical, pharmacy, wheelchair, vehicle); week/month; shared by both personas; change alerts.
3. Provider & Company Directory — doctors, pharmacies, wheelchair suppliers, transport/vehicle services.
4. Referral Tracker — status and expiry dates.
5. Records Transfer Tracker — requested → sent → received → confirmed; alerts if records due before an appointment haven't arrived.
6. Prescription Management — refill schedules, linked pharmacies.
7. Wheelchair & Equipment — ongoing repair/maintenance logs, warranties, contacts, service appointments.
8. Van & Transport — repair/maintenance logs, logistics, service records, fuel tracking.
9. Receipt & Expense Tracking — capture fuel/maintenance receipts, categorise for tax, year-end export.
10. Navigation Integration — one-tap navigate from any appointment; Apple Maps default on iOS, best maps on Android; multi-stop day view for the driver.
11. Proactive Reminders — context-aware alerts before things become urgent.
12. Dual-Persona Views — full picture for the person in care; task-focused run for the assistant/driver.

## Brand tone
Warm, supportive, encouraging, calm. A life companion, not a clinical tool. Affirming, human language — never bureaucratic.

## Strategic principles
- Reduce cognitive load — complexity held by the app, not the user.
- Surface what matters before it becomes urgent.
- Treat the user as capable and in control, not a patient to be managed.
- One view covers everything.
- Appointment changes are first-class events, handled gracefully.

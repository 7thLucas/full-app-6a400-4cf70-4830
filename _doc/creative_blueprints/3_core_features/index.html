Core Features

Built for Person in Care Assistant / Driver

👩‍⚕️ Provider Directory Doctors, pharmacies, wheelchair & transport companies
📋 Referral Tracker Active referrals, status updates, and expiry alerts
💊 Prescription Management Prescriptions, refill schedules, pharmacy contacts
♿ Wheelchair & Equipment Ongoing repair logs, warranties, service appointments
🚐 Van & Transport Maintenance logs, repairs, fuel tracking, service history
🧾 Receipt & Tax Tracking Capture fuel & maintenance receipts — categorised for year-end tax deductions
🔔 Proactive Alerts Smart heads-up before things become urgent
👥 Dual-Persona Views Full care picture for the person; task view for the assistant/driver

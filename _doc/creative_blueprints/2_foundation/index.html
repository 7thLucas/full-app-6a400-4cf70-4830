The Problem

Complex care has no single view

Multiple doctors, pharmacies, referrals, wheelchair companies, and a van that needs constant maintenance — all with changing appointments and ongoing repairs. Fuel and maintenance receipts need to be captured for tax deductions but end up lost. The cognitive load falls on two people (the person in care and their assistant/driver) with no shared system, and the risk of missing something consequential is constant.

The Solution

One hub. Two views. Proactive.

A single companion app for both the person in care and their assistant/driver — each with a view tailored to their role. All appointments, referrals, prescriptions, repairs, and receipts in one place. Receipts captured at the point of transaction for year-end tax reporting. Proactive alerts surface what needs attention before it becomes urgent.

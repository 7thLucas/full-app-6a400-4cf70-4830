The Problem

Complex care has no single view

Multiple doctors, pharmacies, referrals, wheelchair suppliers, and a maintenance-dependent vehicle — all with changing appointments and deadlines. The full cognitive load of tracking it falls on one person, with no unified system. Appointments shift without warning. Referrals expire. Prescriptions lapse. The stakes are high.

The Solution

One companion. Proactive. Warm.

A single app that holds every thread — all providers, appointments, prescriptions, referrals, equipment, and transport. It surfaces what needs attention before it becomes urgent, and speaks with a warm, encouraging voice. The app keeps ahead of things so the user doesn't have to.
